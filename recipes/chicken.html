Oyster Sauce Chicken

Ingredients


  • 4 large bone-in chicken thighs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce

steps to make it

  1. Partially freeze the steak for 15 to 20 minutes (this will make it easier to cut). Cut the steak into thin strips.
  2. Wipe the mushrooms with a damp cloth and cut into slices to make 1 cup
  3. Heat up 2 tablespoons of oil or margarine a large skillet or frying pan with medium-high heat. Brown the meat, turning over once.
  4. Add the mushrooms and the baby corn. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Combine the soy sauce with the beef broth. Set aside.
